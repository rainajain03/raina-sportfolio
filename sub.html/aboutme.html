Hi!
I'm Raina

Born and brought up in Mumbai, since I was young, I've always had a knack for art and design. It was one of the things that got me through school. Design is everywhere around us, from billboards to mobile covers. As a design student, I understand the blood, sweat and tears that go behind every illustration. It fascinates me as to how design has evolutionized itself over various decades and encourages me to know more.
I wish to communicate with people through the visuals I make.
